Students

I have chaired or co-chaired sixteen graduate committees at the University of Idaho and West Virginia University, and served as a committee member for more than two dozen additional students. Nearly all of my advisees coauthor the research they help produce.

Prospective students: I welcome inquiries from students interested in commodity and energy markets, risk management, and applied econometrics. Strong quantitative preparation and curiosity about how real markets work matter more than a fixed background. Please email me a short note about your interests along with your CV.
